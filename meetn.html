AboutNoureen

Noureen Aziz is a passionate digital strategist and marketing professional, dedicated to helping UAE businesses thrive online through impactful social media campaigns, compelling content, and conversion-focused websites. Originally from Karachi, Pakistan, Noureen brings a dynamic blend of creativity and strategic thinking to every project she undertakes.

Currently serving as a Digital Marketing Specialist at Pearl Coast International in Dubai, she has successfully led digital initiatives for both local and international brands. Her expertise spans across brand marketing, campaign management, partnerships, and business development.

With an academic background that includes a Master's in Public Administration from Karachi University and an MPhil in Business Management (Marketing) from the Institute of Business Management, Noureen blends theory with hands-on experience. Her journey in digital marketing began in 2019 as a freelance content writer and strategist, quickly evolving into a career filled with remarkable milestones—including representing her company at international expos and speaking engagements.

Noureen is also a certified LinkedIn Content & Creative Design professional and has taught social media marketing as visiting faculty, underscoring her commitment to knowledge sharing and industry innovation.

Driven by faith, determination, and a global mindset, Noureen continues to inspire businesses to build meaningful digital footprints and achieve lasting growth.
